Topics

 

Features

Empower donors to give generously, allowing them to choose their preferred giving channel.

DevMan  integrates to various online giving gateways, including:

•Payfast

•PayGate

 

Channels include:

•VCS credit / debit card transactions

•Secure instant deposit (SiD)

•SnapScan

•Zapper

•PayPal

•Mobicred

 

Once the donation is processed, the data is automatically recorded on DevMan.

Please contact Support for more information.

 

How it works

A payment portal provides a secure environment for financial transactions.

•The donor submits a donation commitment via your website.

•The donor is then routed to the secure payment portal which processes the payment.

•The portal handles the transaction and pushes the donation details to DevMan.

Talk to your DevMan representative about setting up your online giving integration.
