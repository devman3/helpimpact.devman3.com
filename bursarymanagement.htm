Overview

 

Transform your bursary management process with configurable, versatile workflow designed to streamline your tasks.

Use the DevMan bursary module to launch, measure and grow your student awards program.

 

Topics

 

Features

•Each student has a bursary profile for every year they are a beneficiary.

•Link students to their institution, mentor and funder.

•Track progress of your bursaries using life-cycle or milestones.

•Communicate with specific groups of bursary holders.

•Have students submit results online.

•Keep a record of past and current qualifications.

•Seamless rollover of bursaries to a new academic year.
