Tips and best practice

•Enter as much accurate information as you can, as this will save you time later.

•Avoid duplications by checking whether the organisation is already on the system. (View the tutorials under Searching for more advanced options)

•If an organisation has a long name and uses an acronym, add it to speed up future access.
