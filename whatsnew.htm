New features in DevMan

 

DevMan self-service portal

Say goodbye to the hassle of capturing SARS data.

Donors can now update their own tax details using the new self-service portal.

Simply add the portal link to bulk emails and to your website.

With one click, donors can sign in using their email address and password.

To learn more, please see DevMan portal.

 

SARS 2023 IT3(d) submission requirements

Effective 1 March, 2024, SARS requires approved section 18A organisations to complete and file the new IT3(d) returns.

The return will include information on donors to whom s18A receipts have been issued with the relevant donation amounts.

DevMan has designed and implemented a SARS IT3 export file using the HTTPS channel for easy submission.

To learn more, please see SARS IT3 exports.

New requirements for users sending bulk email

Effective 1 February, 2024, Google and Yahoo will introduce additional spam-blocking logic.

It necessitates any domain that sends over 5 000 emails in a 24-hour period to meet these new verification standards.

To proactively ensure your delivery rates remain unaffected, please review the requirements to learn how to comply.

SARS 2023 section 18A receipt requirements

Effective 1 March 2023, SARS requires additional information to be included in section 18A receipts issued to donors by approved section 18A institutions.

DevMan is designed to assist you to gather this information via an automated self-service portal.

To learn more, please see SARS 2023 section 18A receipt format below or contact the support team.
