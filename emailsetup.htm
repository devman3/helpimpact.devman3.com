Set up email

•The email address you wish to send emails from has to be verified to give DevMan permission to use it. You will need to trigger the email verification request.

•You will receive an email from Amazon Web Services with the subject line that reads: Amazon Web Services - Email Address Verification Request in region Europe (Ireland).

•Once received, click on the link in the body of the email. A page in the browser to confirm the verification. Please view the tutorial below to see how.

•NOTE: You do not need to open an AWS account.

Set up email for bulk mailer compliance

As of 1 February, 2024, Google and Yahoo will require additional verification for domains that send more than 5 000 emails in a 24-hour period.

These domains will be marked as high-volume marketers and will need to have a custom MAIL-FROM and SPF record to ensure their emails are not blocked or marked as spam.

 

In general, the DevMan system uses the devman.co.za domain to send your emails. In this case, no action is required, as devman.co.za is fully compliant.

However, if you are using your own domain to send emails through DevMan, some action may be needed. DevMan support will be in contact soon.

 

If you would like to have the option to send emails from your own domain using our product, you will need to make some changes to your DNS settings to activate the verification.

Please contact DevMan support to initiate this request.
