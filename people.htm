Overview

 

This section will assist you to capture, save and edit your contacts' details and profile.

Links can be added to other profiles on the database.

 

Topics

 

Features

•Keep an up-to-date record of all your contacts in an organisation.

•DevMan's geographic information system (GIS) seamlessly integrates with Google Maps, making data reports more accurate.

•View interactive maps pinpointing locations.

•DevMan supports GIS databases for South Africa, Australia and USA.

 

Tips and best practice

•Enter as much accurate information as you can, as this will save you time later.

•Avoid duplications by checking whether a person is already on the system. (View the tutorials under Searching for more advanced options)

How GIS works

When you enter your contact's address, country and postal code, the region is automatically populated with the correct region from the GIS database.

Up to three can be added: physical, postal, mailing or other.

If you use the Google address lookup feature, the address will be filled in automatically.

If you use the GIS auto feature, linked projects and financial records will automatically add the locality based on the linked contact.

If the address changes on the contact, linked projects set to auto will be updated.
