Overview

 

Use this module for end-to-end management of your entire grantmaking cycle.

Reduce data capture through use of customized online forms, enhance team collaboration on your award process, and systematize your M&E data collection.

 

Topics

 

Features

•Streamline decision making using public online pages to screen and process applications

•Improve team productivity with grant workflow steps and rules

•Surface key information via customisable reporting dashboards

•Manage financials from budgeting through to payment

•Collect monitoring and evaluation information with integrated online forms

 

•Process guide

The Process guide guide is a basic outline of a workflow scenario to help you to customise your DevMan process, and will assist in planning the initial rollout.
