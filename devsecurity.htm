Tips and best practice

We recommend that you assign a senior member of your team to care for this important duty.

To ensure your data is protected, always keep devices and browsers up to date, and be vigilant at all times.

It is crucial that all users:

•Change their passwords regularly.

•Use a strong password with 8 or more characters (alphanumeric, preferably with caps and symbols).
