Actions due

Overview

 

By setting up reminders, you can keep track of which activities are due and which have been completed. Letter reminders are set up as part of the project life cycle.

 

Topics

 

Once you have captured a new proposal, you may receive a reminder in My inbox on the home page, reminding you to send an acknowledgment letter.  Thereafter you'll need to complete your reminder.

 

You can also create your own reminders.  Examples of reminders include: assessment, general reminder, progress report, send letter and send SMS.

 

A reminder can be added to a contact record, or to a project.  Examples of reminders include: assessment, general reminder, progress report, send letter and send SMS.  

However, you can add your own type of reminder.

 

Create a reminder

 

1.Search for and select your contact or project. (You can also create reminder from within an Interaction)
To learn

 

2.In the profile screen go to Add > Activity > Reminder.

 

3.In the Reminder details screen, type in a description and choose an Action due from the drop-down menu.  

 

4.Select a Type e.g. Interim, Final in the case of Progress reports.

 

5.Set the date of your reminder and check that all other details are correct. Save > Save and close.

 

6.Once you have set your reminder, when it becomes due it will appear on your home page in My Inbox.  You will then need to complete your reminder i.e. carry out the activity.

 

 

Complete a reminder

1.There are two ways to complete a reminder. Firstly, simply click on the Activities due in My Inbox on the Home Page.

 

2.Or, you can search for and select your project or contact, and go to View > Activities > Activities due in the profile screen.

 

3.In the grid, select the reminder you wish to complete.

 

4.Remember that this is a reminder, so in order to carry out the activity associated with that reminder you need to complete it. In the Reminder details screen, click in the box next to Completed? Save > Save and close, or click on Complete reminder if you wish to send an email or letter.

 

5.In the next screen you can carry out your activity. Change the date if necessary.  

 

6.For a progress report, you may wish to attach a document, by clicking "Add".  For more information see Including documents with dossier.Or you can type the information into the Details block.  In the case of letters, for more information see Communication.

 

7.Once complete, click Save > Save and close. Once you have completed your reminder, you will notice that the number of reminders in My Inbox has decreased.
