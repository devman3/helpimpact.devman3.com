DevManGo 

Send the event link for DevManGo to all co-ordinators.

At the event, use the app on your tablet or phone to:

  • register all attendees,
  • confirm or update contact details,
  • register walk-ins - those invited but who have not accepted,
  • add new attendees.

On the Event summary page, select Edit then go to Options.

Add one or more co-ordinator by searching for and selecting their name. 

Click here to email the event link for DevManGo. 

  • clicking on the link, 
  • using the code in the app dashboard, 
  • or scanning the QR code. 

From the email, open the event on DevManGo by: 

Use Search to find the person registering.

To view and register walk-ins, tick Invited.

Click through on the person's name to view contact details and mark as attended. 

Click on Add attendee to add additional people not on your list. 

Slide over to mark as attended.

Check and update contact details if necessary. 

Close to go back to the event.
