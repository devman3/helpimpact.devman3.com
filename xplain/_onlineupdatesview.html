View recent online updates

When a person completes their online update, DevMan will match their email address to an existing profile.

You can view all recent updates and check the details.

On the Persons tab, select Online updates > Recent updates.

Click through on the person to view details.

If the email address is not found on DevMan, the assigned person will be notified.

They can add the person's name and email address to DevMan, and send them an online update request.
