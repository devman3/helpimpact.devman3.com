Issue section 18A receipts in bulk

At the end of the financial year, issue section 18A receipts for all donations.

Email them directly to the donors or download them and send from your own email.

Please contact the Support team to set up your template.

Go to Finance > 18A receipts > Transactions due.

Select Transaction date if you wish to issue certificates on a monthly basis. 

Select Transaction status and Donation type from the dropdown list. 

Section  18A should be ticked.

Anonymous should be unticked. 

You can also filter on any of these. 

Select the correct Tax period. 

Selections made for this example.

Click on Search.

TIP: If you make an incorrect selection, simply click on it and try again.

Select View to see the list and then generate the certificates from there. 

Select Process to go directly to generate the certificates. 

The process has been successfully completed and the receipts can now be generated. 

To email the section 18A receipts directly to the donors, click on Email, select the Template, and then Email.

Select View/Download  if you wish to use the following options:

  • print the s18A receipts,
  • email them from a different email address,
  • or use any other distribution method you prefer.

The section 18A receipts have been issued.
