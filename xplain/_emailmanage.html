Manage email

In this tutorial, see how to:

  • view sent emails
  • check delivery status
  • resend a failed email

Emails can be viewed from the person, organisation or project page by selecting View then Letters.

Click on the email to view it.

To view all emails sent, go to the Communication tab and select Email then View.

Click on Refresh if you wish to update the data.

Click on Itemised list to view individual emails.

This grid view shows important statistics about your email delivery.

NOTE: For more details of what each Delivery status means, please view the table directly below this tutorial.

Make use of the grid filters to refine your search. 

Use the Status dropdown menu to view emails that have failed, and then you can resend them.

If you are happy that the email address is correct, select the email and then Update to pending. It will be resent.
