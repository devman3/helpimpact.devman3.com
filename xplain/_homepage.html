Home page

When you log in, you will be taken to the Home page.

There are three core menu structures.

  • Tabs provide access to key areas of DevMan.
  • Submenus display on the left relative to the tab you have selected.
  • Sidebar menus are available wherever you see a small arrow on the submenu.

A handy breadcrumb trail tracks where you have worked and allows you to quickly go back.

My to do list

A list of items due for your attention over the next seven days and visible only to you.

TIP: Click  on the refresh button to get the latest details.

Weekly activity

A summary of all activities for the last seven days.

Favourites

Saved shortcuts to pages you were working on.

TIP: To view these, simply click through.

Dashboard

High level views of key statistics.

The drop-down menu provides a rich list of predefined views.

TIP: See Dashboards in the help for more details.
