Search advanced

Here is an example showing how to add three filters to search across multiple fields.

The filters you create can be saved for re-use and shared with colleagues.

Search advanced is conveniently available where needed.

Build reports, spreadsheets and dashboards with criteria from a wide variety of data sources.

We are looking for bursary students...

  • with bursaries approved in the current year,
  • who are studying in the Eastern and Western Cape,
  • and who are staying in a self-catering facilty.

TIP:

Multiple selections can be made on these data fields.

97 meet the criteria.

Next we search for which of these students

  • have received payments in that period.

513 received payments.

96 of the 97  in the first group received payments.

Finally, which of these students 

  • are sponsored by a foundation.

177 funded by a foundation

9 meet all criteria

Enter a name if you wish to use this search again, then save.

Once the search is complete, save it for re-use and allow others to see it by checking the Make public box.

Now you can view the results and build reports, spreadsheets and view dashboards.

You have now seen how to use a few of the many features of the Search advanced function.

Don't forget that this is available in all areas of DevMan.
