Overview

 

Easily apply a series of changes to a group of records by using the bulk update feature.

Updates can be made to people, organisations, projects, bursaries and financials.

 

Topics

 

Features

•Flag a set of people or organisations

•Perform regular housekeeping and clear out unused data

•Move a set of bursaries or projects to a particular status

•Manage the status of pledge and payment records

Tips for importing your lists successfully

•Download the template from the Event summary page - Invitees > Add via > Importer > Download template.

•Copy and paste your data onto the template in the correct columns and save.

•Make sure that all cells are in text format.

•Delete all other data that is not in these columns.
