Add an interaction by email

Send emails
To automatically create an interaction in DevMan, simply Bcc a DevMan system email address when sending emails from your mail application.
Contacts that are recognised by means of the email address will be represented on the interaction as:

•The email recipients will show in the ‘With’

•You, the sender will show as the ‘By’

•Any people copied in (CC’d) will show in the ‘With’

•Any attachments will be included

•The interaction will be flagged as an email sent

If any email address is not recognised, you will be notified by email.

You can then add the person to DevMan and manually link them to the interaction in the 'With' field.

 

Forward emails

Important emails received from contacts can also be forwarded to DevMan to log the interaction.

From your email application select 'Forward', remove any signatures and send to your DevMan system email address.

•You, the recipient, will show as the 'With'

•The sender will show as the 'By'

•Any people copied in (CC’d) will show in the ‘With’

•Any attachments will be included

•The interaction will be flagged as an email received

If any email address is not recognised, you will be notified by email.

You can then add the person to DevMan and manually link them to the interaction in the 'By' or 'With' field.

 

Your system may need to be configured.

Please contact DevMan support to confirm the email setup for your organisation.
DevMan will check for emails every 30 minutes during the day.

 

Is there a limit to the size of an attachment when using the email to interaction feature?

There is a strict 20MB limit on file uploads for various reasons such as storage and security.

This limit is standard across popular email providers.

However, larger attachments can be added to the interaction manually once it  has been created.

 

How do I automatically link an interaction to a project?

When adding an interaction to DevMan by email, you can set it up to automatically link to the correct project.

Contact DevMan support to enable this feature and configure the subject line to suit your needs.

TIP: When sending an email, add the project number at the end of the subject line in the following format: [A/123456] (where 123456 represents the project number).
