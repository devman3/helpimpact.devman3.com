Summary and edit pages

Toggle between summary / edit mode on relevant pages.

•Summary gives a high-level static view of key information.

•Edit is a detailed view where you can change (add/edit/delete) the content.

 

Saving your work

There are two options from the Save menu:

•Save and stay on page to save work done and then continue with further changes. The page will refresh and may show additional fields.

•Save and close when you have completed your entry. The summary page will now display.

 

FAQ
How do I manage my bookmarks and favourites?

On your Home page, select My settings > Favourites. This will display a list of all your saved favourites.

Click on the row you want to change to view the pop-up box, and there you can update the name of the link, or delete it.

 

How do I go back to the previous page?

Use the breadcrumb trail to go back to a previous profile.

To navigate back to the previous page, use the DevMan back button and not the browser's back option.
